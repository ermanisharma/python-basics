Pandas::
1. Abbreviation to word 'Panel Data'
   Panel - data that is derived from economic obeservations
2. Objective : To identify relationship of a parameter with other parameter
3. Developed by Wes McKinney, in year 2008
4. Open Source library

 Dependable & independable variables

 Example : Any eCommerce website (Walmart)
 -- Orders are placed for items in website //dependable variable
 -- geographical location of customers, customer age, customer income, customer education
